If you live in private rental accommodation, you can use this service to tell the government about problems with the accommodation or with your landlord. You can:

  • register for the service
  • report an issue with your accommodation
  • check the status of previous reports

Telling us about these problems means that the government can investigate and, if necessary, force your landlord to fix the problems. If they don't, they could receive a fine or even end up in prison.

Registering takes around 5 minutes.
